Your Privacy is Protected

LadyQueens does not collect, store, or process any critical or sensitive personal data. We respect your privacy and designed our app to function without needing your personal information.

Welcome to LadyQueens. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy explains our minimal data practices.

Important: LadyQueens is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any sensitive personal information, financial data, health information, or precise location data.

No Critical Data Collection

LadyQueens is designed to respect your privacy. We want to be completely transparent about what we do and don't collect:

Data We DO NOT Collect:

  • No Personal Identifiers - We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or physical address
  • No Financial Information - We don't collect payment details, credit card information, or banking data
  • No Health Data - We don't collect health information, medical records, or fitness data
  • No Precise Location - We don't collect your precise GPS location or track your movements
  • No Sensitive Information - We don't collect racial, ethnic, political, or religious information
  • No Biometric Data - We don't collect fingerprints, face recognition, or other biometric data

1. Information We Collect

LadyQueens collects minimal, non-personal information to provide basic app functionality:

Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about how the app is used to improve our services. This data cannot be traced back to you personally.

Device Information (Non-Personal)

We may collect general device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes only.

App Performance Data

We collect crash reports and performance metrics to fix bugs and improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Fixing technical issues and bugs
  • Understanding general usage patterns (anonymous and aggregated)
  • Ensuring compatibility with different devices

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any personal information because we don't collect it in the first place. The anonymous, aggregated data we collect may be shared with:

  • Analytics Providers - For understanding app usage patterns (anonymous data only)
  • Service Providers - Who help us maintain and improve the app

No Personal Data Sharing: Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to share with third parties, advertisers, or other companies.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Even though we don't collect personal data, we want you to know your rights:

  • Right to Know - You have the right to know what data we collect (as outlined in this policy)
  • Right to Access - You can request information about any data associated with your device
  • Right to Delete - You can request deletion of any anonymous data associated with your device
  • Right to Opt-Out - You can disable data collection in app settings

5. Data Security

While we don't collect sensitive information, we still implement security measures to protect the limited data we do handle:

  • Secure data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited data retention periods

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
