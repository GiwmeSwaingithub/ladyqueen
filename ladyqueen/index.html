NO DATA COLLECTED

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our application ("App") handles your information. We want to be transparent about our practices so you can feel confident using our App.

By using our App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App operates with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track how you use the App, what features you access, or how long you spend in the App.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or any other device-specific information.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect, store, or track your geographic location.
  • No Payment Information: Our App does not process payments and therefore does not collect any financial information.

Compliance with Laws & Policies

Our Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): Even though we don't collect data, we respect the principles of GDPR including transparency, data minimization, and user rights.
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): California residents have specific privacy rights which we respect by not collecting any personal information.
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act): Our App is safe for children as we do not collect any data from any users, regardless of age.
  • Google Play Store Policies: We comply with Google's User Data policies by clearly disclosing our no-data-collection practices.

Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The App functions entirely offline and does not connect to external servers or analytics platforms.

If we ever decide to incorporate third-party services in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and obtain user consent where required by law.

Your Rights

Even though we don't collect your data, we want you to know your privacy rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request confirmation that we are not collecting your data.
  • Right to Deletion: Since we don't store your data, there's nothing to delete.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can stop using the App at any time, and no data will be retained.
  • Right to Notification: We will notify users if our data practices change in the future.
